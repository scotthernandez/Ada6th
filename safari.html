Safari Adventure

San Diego Zoo Safari Park

Essential Info

Address:

15500 San Pasqual Valley Rd, Escondido, CA

Hours:

9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Arrive early!)

Parking is $20. Arriving by 8:45 AM is recommended to get through security quickly.

Ada's 6th Birthday Schedule

9:00 AM

Africa Tram

Head straight here first! It's a 25-minute safari through the savanna.

10:30 AM

Walkabout Australia

Walk among free-roaming kangaroos and wallabies.

11:30 AM

Lorikeet Landing

Hand-feed nectar to the birds. They will land on your arm!

12:15 PM

Picnic Lunch

Eat near the Village Playground so Ada can run around.

Pro-Tips for a 6 Year Old

Hidden Tiger Nook

In the **Tiger Trail**, look for the small netted rope climb. It leads to a secret viewing window right where the tigers sleep!

Petting Kraal

Near the entrance, Ada can brush and pet the goats. A great "low speed" interaction for a break.

Cool Zones

If it's hot, look for the spritzing palm trees near Lion Camp to cool down.

Getting Around

The park is large and hilly. Consider these tips:

  • **Wear comfortable shoes:** There is a lot of walking involved.
  • **Download the App:** Use the San Diego Zoo app for real-time maps and show times.
  • **Shuttle Bus:** There is a shuttle that runs from the bottom of the park (near the tigers) back up to the entrance area.
