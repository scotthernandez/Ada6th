## Itinerary & Activities

### Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration
*   **Drive down:** Consider leaving early to beat traffic.
*   **Check-in:** Head to The Welk in Escondido.
*   **Sushi Dinner:** (See Dining options below for reservations/choices).

### Day 2: Safaris & Tide Pools
*   **Morning: San Diego Zoo Safari Park**
    *   **Location:** 15500 San Pasqual Valley Rd, Escondido (15-20 mins from hotel).
    *   **Hours:** 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Arrive right at 9 AM to beat the heat and crowds).
    *   **Parking:** Starts at $20 (free for Wildlife Alliance members).
    *   **Ada's Safari Schedule (6 yo):** 
        *   **9:00 AM:** Arrive and head straight to the **Africa Tram** (25 min ride) before the lines get long.
        *   **10:30 AM:** Walk over to **Walkabout Australia** to see kangaroos up close.
        *   **11:30 AM:** Visit **Lorikeet Landing** to let Ada feed the colorful birds (liquid nectar available for purchase).
        *   **12:15 PM:** Lunch break near the **Village Playground** or **Elephant Play Yard** so she can run and play.
        *   **1:30 PM:** Check out the **Tiger Trail** (there's a log-walk/ropes area great for kids).
        *   **2:30 PM:** Ride the **Conservation Carousel** to wrap up before it gets too hot!
*   **Afternoon: Tide Pools (If time permits)** - See Day 3 for tide pool details.

### Day 3: Coastline & Marine Life
*   **Morning: Whale Watching from Point** (Check maps for exact departure point).
*   **Late Morning/Afternoon: La Jolla Coastline**
    *   **La Jolla Shores Tide Pools**
        *   *Best viewing:* At or just before low tide (check tide charts). Dike Rock (north of Scripps Pier) is a great spot.
        *   *Parking:* La Jolla Shores Beach parking lot, El Paseo Grande Street, or residential streets near La Jolla Cove (often 2-3 hour limits).
    *   **UCSD Scripps Pier**
        *   *Note:* The pier itself is closed to the public for research, but you can walk under/around it at the Dike Rock tide pools. 
        *   *Parking:* Free 3-hour parking at Birch Aquarium, or use ParkMobile for campus lots.
    *   **Bird Rock Waterfront**
        *   *Location:* South of La Jolla.
        *   *Activities:* Walk the paved coastal path, visit Calumet Park for bluff views, or check out La Jolla Hermosa park. Lots of local shops and cafes.
        *   *Parking:* Free street parking in the residential neighborhood.
